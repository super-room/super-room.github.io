Super Room

Generate thousands of rooms in a single prompt

Mikul Rai, Francis Engelmann, Ian Huang, Elisabetta Fedele, Leonidas Guibas

Designing realistic and controllable rooms from prompts remains a challenge, as current approaches either sacrifice geometric fidelity or lack fine-grained controllability.

We address this by generating variations of existing rooms that preserve object positions and size while allowing within-category shape variation and texture changes.
